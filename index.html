Chapter One

How it all started

I don't remember
what we ordered.

I remember
how happy I felt.

Every ordinary evening
became one I wanted
to remember.

The day I met the girl
who quietly became

my whole world.

Chapter Two

Falling in love

Every place became beautiful…

because you were there.

We weren't just
marking occasions.

We were collecting forever.

Even the rain
didn't mind waiting
for us to finish our walk.

Chapter Three — 19 January 2025

The proposal

On a longtail boat, tucked into a quiet cove in Phi Phi, Thailand.

One knee.

One question.

One yes
that changed everything.

We weren't travelling
anymore.

We were engaged.

Chapter Four

Seychelles

You fell in love
with La Digue.

I fell in love with watching
you fall in love with it.

Just us,
a wooden door,
and nowhere to be.

You always notice
the little things —

the light through the leaves,
the sound of the waves.

You in that orange dress, watching the waves break —
I don't think I've ever seen anything more beautiful.

Thirty feet underwater,
and you still made time

for a heart.
